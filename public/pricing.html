How much does Lodgify cost?
Lodgify pricing starts at $11 per month and varies depending on the total number of rentals and features. You can test our free trial for 7 days before buying a subscription. See the additional pricing details above.
How is the booking fee on the Starter plan calculated?
The booking fee is charged on all bookings, including those received through the website and API connected channels (Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo/HomeAway and Expedia). It’s not charged on any iCal connections.
Who is Lodgify for?
Lodgify is designed to help all types of short term rental businesses grow – from 1 properties to more than 1000.
Can I use Lodgify if I already have a website?
Yes, you can still use Lodgify to manage all your bookings from one place. There are many things you can do with Lodgify such as embed Lodgify's booking engine on your existing website to start receiving direct bookings, sync all your channels with Lodgify's channel manager and use our reservation system and/or calendar to run your short-term rental business.
Can I try Lodgify without paying?
Yes, you can fully test our software for up to 7 days prior to any payment. We don’t require a credit card number to test our software. Create your website and customize it first, before deciding on your Lodgify plan.
Start your free trial
Is there a set-up fee?
No, there is no set-up fee when joining Lodgify.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can change to a different plan at any time.
How do I pay the subscription plan?
We automatically charge your credit card according to your billing cycle.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Although we will miss you, you can cancel your subscription at any time.
Do you offer any discounts?
Yes, we offer a 30% discount on our annual and a 35% discount on our two-year plan when they are paid upfront. Additionally, you will also receive a free custom domain with both of these plans.
Are custom plans available?
Yes! We provide custom plans for multi-website or multi-property users with more than 100 vacation rentals. Please contact us and we will provide you with a personalized quote.
Can I use my own domain name?
Of course! You can either use your own domain name registered with an external provider, or register a new domain name via Lodgify.
Do I need a merchant account in order to accept credit card payments?
No you don’t. In order to start accepting credit card payments, all you need is a Stripe or PayPal account – you can register for free.
Do I need a web host or have to download anything?
No. Our plans include unlimited hosting for your vacation rental website. Everything is up in the cloud, so there’s no need to install anything.
